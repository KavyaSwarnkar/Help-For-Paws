Our Mission

At Animal Savior, we are dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and care of injured cows and orphaned calves. Our goal is to create a safe environment where these animals can heal and thrive.

Our Services

Rescue Operations

We respond to emergency cases of injured animals and provide them with the necessary care.

Veterinary Care

Our team of vets ensures the best medical treatment for the animals in our care.

Adoption Services

We help orphaned calves find loving homes through our adoption program.
